The deadline for submitting Special Sessions/Tracks proposals closed last week with around 70 submissions of a high level and a great variety of themes and formats. Once the review has been completed, those Special Sessions/Tracks approved will be added to the list of tracks proposed by the organization, being available to be chosen in the Call for Abstracts, which will open in November. Get your work ready!

body: >-


Seventy proposals were received within the Special Sessions/Tracks Call launched last September. Of these, half are for the organization of interactive sessions and artistic sessions, having been the submissions made by authors from numerous entities belonging to more than 20 different countries around the world. The outcome of the reviews, which are being carried out by the Scientific Committee, will be communicated to the proposers on November 6. The approved sessions/tracks will be included in the list of tracks initially proposed by the organization, and all of them can be selected by those who want to send their communication proposals during the Call for Abstracts that will open in mid-November.
