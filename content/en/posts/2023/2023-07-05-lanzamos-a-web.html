The ESEE-Degrowth Pontevedra 2024 website is now available. In this space you can find all the information about the activities that will take place next year in the framework of this event, which will include two kinds of activities: 

  • On the one hand, the academic activities that will be held between June 18 and 21, 2024 on the occasion of the joint 10th International Degrowth Conference and 15th Conference of the European Society for Ecological Economics (ESEE).
  • On the other hand, citizen-oriented initiatives on degrowth and ecological economics will be held throughout the next year and, more specifically, during the days of the Conference. 

This is a live platform, where we will update all the information about all these activities and you can find relevant content such as instructions for abstracts submission, announcements of registration openings and deadlines, access to abstract submission and registration system, the Conference and the extra-academic activities program, and many others. Soon we will also share access to our social networks so that you do not miss any detail.

We look forward to seeing you in Pontevedra in 2024!
