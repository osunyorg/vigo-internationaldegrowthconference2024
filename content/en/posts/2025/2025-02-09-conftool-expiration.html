ConfTool's licence for the ESEE-Degrowth 2024 conference is about to expire. Those who wish to save any of the information contained in the platform (abstract of a presentation, public list of participants...) should do so in the next few days, before Sunday 16 February 2025.

An overview of the programme with the sessions as well as the titles and authors of the presentations can now be found in the ‘Conference Programme’ section of this website.
