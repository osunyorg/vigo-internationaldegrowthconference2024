We are excited to let you know that registrations for the 5th International Assembly of the Degrowth Movement are now open! 

You can register here: https://my.liberaforms.org/2024-movement-assembly-registration (external link) 

Please note that registration (for both online and in person participation) is mandatory and that everyone is welcome to attend:)

The form itself has all the logistical and practical information you might need. 

However, below are some quick details: 

  • When: 17th of June 09:30-18:00 CEST (plus a convivial celebration afterwards)
  • Where: School of Forestry Engineering, Pontevedra
  • Food: Free vegetarian lunch + snacks and hot beverages!

Lastly, it is a lot of work to organise this event. If you are interested in participating in the organising (you don't need to attend to do so) please do reach out to info@degrowth.net (external link)
