The deadline for submission of abstracts for the ESEE-Degrowth 2024 International Conference was 19 January. More than 930 proposals have been received, a success that has exceeded initial expectations. The review process will begin in the coming days, with the results expected to be communicated to participants in early March.  The event organisers would like to thank all applicants - we look forward to seeing you all in Pontevedra!
