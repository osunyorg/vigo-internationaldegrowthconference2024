David Soto-Oñate, PhD is a Postdoc researcher at the University of Vigo (Post-growth Innovation Lab and ECOBAS). Previously, he was a Fulbright researcher at the Ostrom Workshop (Indiana University) for two years. From the perspectives of Institutional and Ecological Economics, David has published research works on a variety of topics in the fields of economic development and the sustainable governance of resources (fisheries, climate change, circular economy, and oil transport). His main current research endeavor revolves around the polycentric governance of a postgrowth social order. He is also PI of the project ARCA  (external link)(Towards an Appropriate Climate Responsibility) funded by the Spanish Ministry of Science and Innovation.
