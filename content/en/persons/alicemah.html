Alice Mah is Professor of Urban and Environmental Studies at the University of Glasgow. Prior to this she was Professor of Sociology at the University of Warwick. Her research focuses on toxic pollution, environmental justice, and just transformations, the subjects of her two most recent books: Petrochemical Planet (Duke University Press, 2023) and Plastic Unlimited (Polity, 2022).
