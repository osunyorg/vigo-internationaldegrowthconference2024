Poonam is a Maria Zambrano fellow at the University of Vigo. She is an interdisciplinary researcher working at the interface of STS, development studies, and innovation studies. Before this position, Poonam taught Public Policy and Sustainable Development at FLAME University and Indian Institute of Science, India. She is also a Co-PI of the ESRC UK funded GR Epics project hosted at the Institute of Development Studies (IDS), Sussex. Her recent work engages with the debates on Responsible Research and Innovation (RRI) from a Global South perspective. Broadly, her work focuses on understanding knowledge politics, inclusion and justice in socio-technical systems. Her recent work has been published in Energy Research and Social Sciences, Agriculture and Human Values, Technology in Society and the Journal of Responsible Innovation.
