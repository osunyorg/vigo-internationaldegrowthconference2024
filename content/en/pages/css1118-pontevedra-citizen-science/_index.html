This special session will explain the Postgrowth SA: Pontevedra project, a year-long citizen science initiative in Pontevedra involving high school students. They investigated achieving a just ecological transition in the city through four case studies: water pollution, mental health and green spaces, loss of rural lifestyles, and employment. The replicable and scalable methodology used will be detailed, highlighting key findings, strengths, and lessons learnt for future citizen science processes. header_text: >- CSS1118.
