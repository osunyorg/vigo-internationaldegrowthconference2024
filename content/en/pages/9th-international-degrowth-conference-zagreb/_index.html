The 9th International Degrowth Conference will be held in Zagreb (Croatia) between August 29 and September 2, 2023, under the theme "Planet, People, Care: It Spells Degrowth!"

body: >-


This is only a compilation of the most remarkable aspects of the conference. Deeper information about the event can be found at  odrast.hr

Venue

The Conference will be held in different locations:  ACADEMIC ACTIVITIES

Museum of Contemporary Art  (Muzej za suvremenu umjetnost): Avenija Dubrovnik 17, 10000, Zagreb

Zagreb Fair  (Zagrebački Velesajam): Avenija Dubrovnik 15, 10020, Zagreb
EXTRA-ACADEMIC ACTIVITIES
Center for Youth Ribnjak  (Centar Mladih ribnjak): Park Ribnjak 1, 10000, Zagreb More information

Keynoters

Roland Ngam - Rosa Luxemburg Stiftung

Diana Ürge-Vorsatz - Central European University and IPCC

Paul Stubbs - Institute of Economics

Karin Doolan - University of Zadar

More info about the keynote speakers

Registration

Registration is already closed. If you are interested in attending but you have not registered in time, please contact  support@odrast.hr .  The  afternoon keynote lectures and the rest of the afternoon sessions are FREE and open to everyone.
