  • The Opening Session will be held at the Pazo da Cultura of Pontevedra on Tuesday 18 June 2024 from 16.00h (CEST time). More information can be found here. 
  • To attend the activities of the conference it will be necessary to register at the points and times provided for this purpose, which will be:
    · Pazo da Cultura (main hall) - Tuesday 18 June, 13.30-16.00h
    · Escola de Enxeñería Forestal (‘soto’ hall) - Wednesday 19 June, 08.30-15.00h; Thursday 20 June, 08.30-; Friday 21 June, 
    When you register you will receive a recycled paper bag containing: meal tickets (if you have previously booked them); a postcard with QR codes to access the most relevant information; several identification stickers to use throughout the conference, on which you must write your name and the the name of your organisation and stick them on your clothes in a visible place. 
  • Information about the conference programme (sessions, times and locations) can be found at this link.
  • During the whole week, we will also count on many non-academic activities open to the public (no registration is required). Some will be developed in English and others in Spanish/Galician. All the information about these activities can be found here. 
  • There will be a permanent information desk in the Escola de Enxeñería Forestal from Wednesday 19 to Friday 21 June at soto floor.
  • Several volunteers will be available at the event venues. They will wear a blue T-shirt with the conference logo so that they can be easily identified.
