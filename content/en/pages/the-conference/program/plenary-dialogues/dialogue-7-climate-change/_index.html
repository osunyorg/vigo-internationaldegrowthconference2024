Friday 21 June 2024, 15.00h (Plenary tent) with Julia Steinberger (University of Lausanne) & Carlo Buontempo (Director of Copernicus Climate Service of the EU), moderated by Mario Pansera (Universidade de Vigo) This dialogue features Dr. Carlo Buontempo, director of Copernicus, and Professor Julia Steinberger from the University of Lausanne, recently awarded the ERC Synergy Grant REAL. Carlo will discuss the latest research on climate change, while Julia will explore how degrowth can be an effective strategy for climate mitigation and adaptation.
