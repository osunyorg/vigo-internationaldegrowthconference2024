Would you like to participate as a volunteer during next year's conference? We are looking for proactive people interested in the fields of degrowth and ecological economics who want to become part of our team. As a volunteer, you will provide support to the organizing committee in developing the academic and extracurricular activities scheduled for June 2024.

This opportunity is perfect for anyone looking to engage with the degrowth movement, gain valuable organizational skills, connect with like-minded individuals, and become more involved in the research ecosystem.

If you are interested in participating, please contact us at esee-degrowth2024@uvigo.gal.
