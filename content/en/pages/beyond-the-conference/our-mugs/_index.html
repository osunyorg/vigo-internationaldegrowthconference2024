The pottery of Buño (Olería de Buño) is one of the traditional centers of ceramics in the northwest of the Iberian Peninsula. In general, Buño pottery is of very good quality and highly valued compared to that of other centers of production.

In Buño (Malpica - Galicia) pottery activity has been documented since the sixteenth century. The potter's trade is recorded as a traditional occupation from 1510.
