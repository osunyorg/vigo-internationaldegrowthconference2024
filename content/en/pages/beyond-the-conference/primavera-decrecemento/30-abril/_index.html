  • When:
    Tuesday 30 April, at 7.00 p. m.

  • Where:
    Casa das Campás (Rúa de Don Filiberto, Pontevedra)

  • Who:
    Víctor Sánchez e Alejandro Fortuny (Post-Growth Innovation Lab)

Approaching the world of work as a way of structuring our lives is key to understanding the eco-social crisis. To initiate a collective debate, we propose to have an introduction to the work of the eco-socialist theorist André Gorz and a screening of a documentary about his life and work

  1. Introduction to Gorz's work in the context of the world of work - Alejandro Fortuny.
  2. Screening of the film Lettre à G in VO (French) subtitled in Spanish
  3. Debate on ways of rethinking work
