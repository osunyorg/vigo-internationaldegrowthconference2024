  • When:
    Thursday 9 May, at 7.00 p.m.

  • Where:
    Faculty of Fine Arts, space 16 - external link (Maestranza 2, Pontevedra)
  • Who:
    Enxeñería sen Fronteiras - external link
    Presenter: Mario Pansera - external link (Post-Growth Innovation Lab - external link)

In this activity we will address the (re)politicisation of technology as a necessary and constitutive part of degrowth. We will have a presentation on convivial technology by Doctor Mario Pansera and a workshop-exhibition by Enxeñaría se Fronteiras. We will show both the potential of generating convivial technologies with society and practical examples carried out by Enxeñaría sen Fronteiras.
