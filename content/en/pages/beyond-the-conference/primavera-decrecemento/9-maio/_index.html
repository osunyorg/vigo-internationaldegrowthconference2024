When: Thursday 9 May, at 7.00 p.m.

Where: Faculty of Fine Arts, space 16 (Maestranza 2, Pontevedra)
Who: Enxeñería sen Fronteiras Presenter:  Mario Pansera  ( Post-Growth Innovation Lab ) In this activity we will address the (re)politicisation of technology as a necessary and constitutive part of degrowth. We will have a presentation on convivial technology by Doctor Mario Pansera and a workshop-exhibition by Enxeñaría se Fronteiras. We will show both the potential of generating convivial technologies with society and practical examples carried out by Enxeñaría sen Fronteiras.
