  • When:
    Thursday 30 April, at 7.00 p.m.

  • Where:
    Casa das Campás (external link) (Don Filiberto St., Pontevedra)

  • Who:
    Pablo Casares Balsa (MeteoGalicia Atmosphere Technician)
    Moderated by: Begoña de Bernardo Miño (membro da Xunta Directiva da Rede para o Decrecemento EO-Navia, Galiza, O Bierzo (external link) e Presidenta de Véspera de Nada)

Pablo Casares will explain what the IPCC (Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change) is and the most optimistic and pessimistic forecasts that this intergovernmental panel foresees at a global level. He will then talk about the possible climate scenarios that could occur in Galicia (Mediterraneanisation of the climate, drop in temperatures, appearance of cyclones in the Atlantic, reduction of rainfall, increase in drought and snow days...).
