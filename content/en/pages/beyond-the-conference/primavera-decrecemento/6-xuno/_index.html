  • When:
    Thursday 6 June, at 7.00 p. m.

  • Where:
    Museo Provincial (Padre Amoedo Carballo St. 3, Pontevedra)

  • Who:
    Julen Bollain (Mondragon Unibertsitatea), Eduardo Garzón Espinosa (Universidad Autónoma de Madrid) e Eva García Sempere (Izquierda Unida)

This activity aims to put in dialogue two economic-political proposals in the face of planetary limits: the Basic Income and the Guaranteed Work. The event will be moderated by Eva García Sempere and will include presentations on Guaranteed Work by Eduardo Garzón and Universal Basic Income by Julen Bollain. After the presentations, a debate will be opened to the public with questions and answers.
