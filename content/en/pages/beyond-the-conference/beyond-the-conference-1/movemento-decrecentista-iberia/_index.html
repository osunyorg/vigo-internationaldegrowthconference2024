Lecture focused on the vision of Degrowth as a social movement. There will be a review and debate on the social and organisational situation of degrowth in Galicia, the rest of Spain and Portugal. There will also be a debate on the challenges for the future and the best way to increase the social acceptance of degrowth proposals in society, families, companies, administrations...
