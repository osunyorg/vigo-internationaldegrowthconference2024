Presentation of the magazine 15/15\15, an independent media, democratically governed by the subscribers themselves, whose objective is to contribute to the construction of a new culture and a new post-industrial, post-capitalist, post-growth civilisation, adjusted to the limits of the biosphere and oriented to the satisfaction of human needs and those of the rest of Gaia.

body: >- When: mércores 19 de xuño de 2024, 19.15h
Where: Casa das Campás (Rúa de Don Filiberto, Pontevedra)Who: Manuel Casal (founder and coordinator of the magazine), Marga Mediavilla (representative of the editorial board of the journal), Rodrigo Osorio (deputy member of the editorial board and poet), and Laura de la Fuente (poet, actress and singer, collaborator of the journal)
The activity will be carried out in Spanish and/or Galician




Program
Projection of an animated clip presenting the journal

Presentation of the magazine by the coordinator and the representative of the editorial board.
Short poetry recital by collaborating poets
Debate with the audience
