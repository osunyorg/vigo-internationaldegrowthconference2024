We will make a big mural designed and painted collectively that represents Pontevedra and The ESEE-Degrowth 2024 Conference as spaces where we can collectively dream and create new realities that inhabit our imagination.

body: >-


When:  Monday 17 June 2024, 18.00-20.00h / Wednesday 19 , 14.00-17.00h / Thursday20-Friday 21, 15.00-18.30h

Where:  School of Forestry Engineering , Campus A Xunqueira (Pontevedra)
Who: Reclamo Estudio   and Sofía Greaves (Artist and researcher at  Post-Growth Innovation Lab )

The activity will be led by Javier Carrera "Cuco":  https://www.instagram.com/cuco_cuco_cuco_cuco/  and Sofía Greaves ( https://www.sofiagreaves.online/ )

Creation dynamics
Contributing ideas for the co-design before the Conference through this link

Painting : participants will receive a paintbrush and paint to fill in the defined spaces. The chalk draws the collective design and marks the boundaries where to put the colour in a simple and clean way, no need to smudge ;-)
Imagining : within the mural area there will be a table with papers, paints, pencils, etc. where people who wish to do so can write down ideas, reflections, objectives, draw graphic elements inspired by the talks and reports of the Conference, all of this to continue with the design of the mural.


The Dreamed City
