"How to praise out loud" (Como alabar en voz alta) is a 20-minute performance by the dancer and creator Eva Comesaña, where contemporary dance is the language chosen to talk about spirituality in contrast to the cruelty that this brings translated into religion. It mixes the celestial and brutality; prayer and offering with penitence and punishment: dualities that are related and reflit in the piece concerning what surrounds us.
