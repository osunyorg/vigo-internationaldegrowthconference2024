Limited funding is available to help cover conference fees for those in financial need, including participants from the Global South and unemployed individuals. This funding is intended solely for conference fee waivers and will be distributed by the Conference Organisers based on available resources from conference subscriptions.

To apply for a waiver, please follow this link (external link) and fill out the necessary information. Note that applying does not guarantee a waiver, as funds are limited.

 Deadline for applications: 31 March, 2024
