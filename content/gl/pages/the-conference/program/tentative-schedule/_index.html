1º Diálogo Plenario: STS in the context of degrowth: science and technology in the era of colonial capitalist expansion
con Samer Abdelnour (lien externe) (The Univesity of Edimburg) & Annapurna Mamidipudi (lien externe) (Technical University of Berlin), moderado por Tess Doezema (Universitat Autónoma de Barcelona)

2º Diálogo Plenario: Introduction to Ecological Economics and Degrowth
con Joshua Farley (lien externe) (University of Vermont), Giorgos Kallis (lien externe) (Universitat Autónoma de Barcelona) & Brototi Roy (lien externe) (Universitat Autónoma de Barcelona),moderado por Alexandra Köves (lien externe) (Corvinus University of Budapest)

3º Diálogo Plenario: The politics of Materials and the plastic industry
con Ekaterina Chertkovskaya (lien externe) (Lund University) & Alice Mah (lien externe) (University of Glasgow), moderado por Vicenzo Pavone (CSIC)

4º Diálogo Plenario: What Circular Economy?
con Kersty Hobson (lien externe) (University of Cardiff) & Hans-Christian Eberl (lien externe) (European Comission, responsible for Circular Economy in Horizon Europe), moderado por Andrea Genovese (Sheffield University Management School)

5º Diálogo Plenario: Technology and Democracy: The Right to Repair Movement
con Ugo Vallauri (lien externe) & Blanca Callén (lien externe) (Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona), moderado por Javier Lloveras (Universidade de Vigo)

6º Diálogo Plenario: Rethinking Innovation: Alternative approaches for people and planet
con Alejandra Boni (lien externe) (Ingenio – UPV), Adrian Smith (lien externe) (University of Sussex) & Andrea Jiménez (lien externe) (University of Sheffield), moderado por Aviram Sharma (Universidade de Vigo)

7º Diálogo Plenario: Climate Change and Degrowth
con  Julia Steinberger (lien externe) (University of Lausanne) & Carlo Buontempo (lien externe) (Director of Copernicus Climate Service of the EU), moderado por Mario Pansera (Universidade de Vigo)
