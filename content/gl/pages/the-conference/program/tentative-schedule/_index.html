1º Diálogo Plenario : STS in the context of degrowth : science and technology in the era of colonial capitalist expansion
con Samer Abdelnour (The Univesity of Edimburg) & Annapurna Mamidipudi (Technical University of Berlin), moderado por Tess Doezema (Universitat Autónoma de Barcelona)

2º Diálogo Plenario: Introduction to Ecological Economics and Degrowth
con Joshua Farley (University of Vermont), Giorgos Kallis (Universitat Autónoma de Barcelona) & Brototi Roy (Universitat Autónoma de Barcelona), moderado por Alexandra Köves (Corvinus University of Budapest)

3º Diálogo Plenario: The politics of Materials and the plastic industry
con Ekaterina Chertkovskaya (Lund University) & Alice Mah (University of Glasgow), moderado por Vicenzo Pavone (CSIC)

4º Diálogo Plenario: What Circular Economy ?
con Kersty Hobson (University of Cardiff) & Hans-Christian Eberl (European Comission, responsible for Circular Economy in Horizon Europe), moderado por Andrea Genovese (Sheffield University Management School)

5º Diálogo Plenario: Technology and Democracy : The Right to Repair Movement
con Ugo Vallauri & Blanca Callén (Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona), moderado por Javier Lloveras (Universidade de Vigo)

6º Diálogo Plenario: Rethinking Innovation : Alternative approaches for people and planet
con Alejandra Boni (Ingenio – UPV), Adrian Smith (University of Sussex) & Andrea Jiménez (University of Sheffield), moderado por Aviram Sharma (Universidade de Vigo)

7º Diálogo Plenario: Climate Change and Degrowth
con  Julia Steinberger (University of Lausanne) & Carlo Buontempo (Director of Copernicus Climate Service of the EU), moderado por Mario Pansera (Universidade de Vigo)
